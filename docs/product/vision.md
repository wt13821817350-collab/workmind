# WorkMind Vision

## Mission

Empower every enterprise with AI employees.

（让每一家企业，都拥有自己的AI员工。）

---

## Vision

Build an enterprise AI workspace where AI is no longer a chatbot but a real employee capable of completing business tasks.

（打造企业AI工作空间，让AI从聊天机器人进化为真正能够完成工作的数字员工。）

---

## Core Principles

WorkMind is NOT another chatbot.

WorkMind is NOT another RAG platform.

WorkMind focuses on helping enterprises automate repetitive work through AI Employees.

Every feature must solve a real business problem.

Every module must be reusable.

Every workflow must be executable.
